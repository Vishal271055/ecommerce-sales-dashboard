# 🛒 E-Commerce Sales Analysis Dashboard

## 1. Project Title / Headline

🛒 E-Commerce Sales Analysis Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze e-commerce sales performance, customer behavior, product trends, and revenue insights through powerful visualizations and business intelligence techniques.

---

## 2. Short Description / Purpose

The E-Commerce Sales Analysis Dashboard helps businesses track sales performance, monitor customer purchasing trends, analyze product categories, and identify opportunities for revenue growth. The dashboard transforms raw transactional data into meaningful business insights for decision-making.

---

## 3. Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

• 📊 Power BI Desktop – Dashboard development and reporting.

• 📂 Power Query – Data cleaning and transformation.

• 🧠 DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – KPI calculations and business metrics.

• 🔗 Data Modeling – Table relationships and cross-filtering.

• 📈 Interactive Visualizations – KPI Cards, Charts, Maps, Funnel Analysis, and Slicers.

• 📁 File Format – .pbix and .png.

---

## 4. Data Source

Source: E-Commerce Sales Dataset

The dataset contains information related to:

• Orders

• Customers

• Products

• Categories

• Sales Revenue

• Profit

• Geographic Locations

• Order Trends

The data supports detailed analysis of sales performance and customer purchasing behavior.

---

## 5. Features / Highlights

### • Business Problem

E-commerce businesses generate large volumes of transactional data, making it difficult to identify:

• Top-selling products

• High-performing regions

• Customer purchasing trends

• Revenue growth opportunities

• Sales conversion performance

Without visualization, extracting these insights is time-consuming.

---

### • Goal of the Dashboard

The dashboard aims to:

• Monitor overall sales performance.

• Track revenue and profit trends.

• Analyze customer and product behavior.

• Identify high-performing regions.

• Support business decision-making with data-driven insights.

---

### • Walkthrough of Key Visuals

#### KPI Cards

Provides quick access to:

• Total Sales

• Total Profit

• Total Orders

• Overall Business Performance

---

#### Sales Trend Analysis (Line Charts)

Shows sales performance over time and highlights growth patterns, seasonal trends, and fluctuations.

---

#### Product Category Analysis (Pie & Donut Charts)

Visualizes category-wise contribution to overall sales and revenue.

---

#### Geographic Sales Analysis (Map Visual)

Displays sales distribution across different locations, helping identify top-performing regions.

---

#### Sales Funnel Analysis

Tracks sales stages and conversion performance to understand customer journey effectiveness.

---

#### Product Performance (Column Charts)

Compares product and category performance based on sales and profitability.

---

#### Interactive Filters (Slicers)

Allows users to dynamically filter the dashboard for deeper analysis.

---

### • Business Impact & Insights

#### Revenue Growth

Identify products and categories driving maximum revenue.

#### Regional Performance

Understand which locations contribute most to business success.

#### Customer Insights

Analyze purchasing behavior to improve marketing strategies.

#### Better Decision Making

Support strategic planning through data-driven insights.
